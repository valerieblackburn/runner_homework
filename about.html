About

I started running in the Spring of 2012. A friend of mine was training for the Boston Marathon. While I was just looking to do a 5-K, her ability pushed me to go further. That same Spring I ran a 10-K at the Redbud Classic and a half-marathon in the Oklahoma City Memorial Marathon.

In 2013, I ran two more half-marathons in Oklahoma City. The Oklahoma City Memorial Marathon in May and the Hits marathon in October.

At the beginning of 2014, I began training for my first marathon.
