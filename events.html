Events

Oklahoma City Memorial Marathon

A local 5K, half-marathon and marathon held every May to commemorate the Oklahoma City bombing. The race is held in downtown Oklahoma City

Redbud Classic

A local 5K, 10K, wheelchair event and 2 mile walk held every April to welcome Spring. The race includes various live music bands and weaves through the Nichols Hills neighborhood.

Rt 66 Marathon

The Williams Route 66 is a local 5K run and walk, half-marathon and marathon held every November in Tulsa, Oklahoma.
